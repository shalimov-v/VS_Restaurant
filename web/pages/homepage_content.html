Lucky Shot Restaurant

“Lucky Shot” – a fine dine restaurant that offers a wide range of exotic meats. Our non-vegetarian, poultry and fish dishes are guaranteed to satisfy the taste of any gourmet. You have arrived in our city and you are unfamiliar with restaurants in it. Many restaurants in the city deserve your attention, but we recommend “Lucky Shot” restaurant. This poultry restaurant is located in the city centre, a few minutes away from most beautiful and interesting places of our city. Our restaurant reflects a hunting house with fur decor solid wood furniture and an open fire.

The restaurant opened in 2002. The residents of our city have the opportunity to dive into the hunting life of the historic City Centre. Throughout the years, restaurant “Lucky Shot” has become part of the city. Many clients have become regular guests, who we are glad to see for countless years in our restaurant. Our restaurant has repeatedly received several prizes, as well as employees have won various culinary competitions.
